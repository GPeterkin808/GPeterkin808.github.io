Gabe's 30 Day Dance Program: Functional Flava

About The Program:

Hello! My name is Gabriel Peterkin, and I am the creator behind Functional Flava - the 30-Day Dance Program taking the world by storm! Meant for beginners, this program is designed to introduce the essentials, fundamentals, and necessities of modern dance styles. In four weeks, concepts such as footwork, isolations, flava (essentially the style and swagger behind the moves) and so much more will be covered. Careful consideration and thought was placed into progression of the program, with multiple step-by-step tutorials for all moves and choreography present. Featuring daily goals, tried-and-true warmup essions, and online live dance sessions and mentors, Functional Flava takes the guesswork and anxiety out of the dance equation, allowing you to focus on your flava. So many programs nowadays tend to say they are for beginners, but jump to intermediate level choreography and difficult air flares within a week or two. Functional Flava understands what it's like to try and become a new dancer and the fears associated with it. I'm here to tell you that you can become the dancer you've always dreamed to be, and to start by building a solid foundation with Functional Flava.

In-Depth Program:

Week 1: The Fundamentals

We begin with the essentials of dance. Footwork, mindset, and musicality are the topics are focused on during this week. Simple moves such as the Arm Wave and Chest Pop are also taught.

Week 2: Fluidity: Popping & Locking

The history of popping and locking is a crucial one to any good dancer. The quick flex and release of the muscles is taught to show the basics of how dancers like Fik-Shun and Michael Jackson came to be.

Week 3: Grooves: Hip Hop

My favorite week focuses on grooves, cool moves, and an exhibition of flava. Swag is necessary here, so be ready for the most crucial week.

Week 4: Isolations: Krump

Just like a bomb, this week focuses on the tics and booms of dance. Know when to be sharp and when to be powerful. The seamless switching in between the two is what made Les Twins, and it can make you too.
